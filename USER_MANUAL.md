## 1. Introduction

Date Jar allows you and your partner (or your friend group!) to create shared collections of activity ideas. When you're ready for a date or hangout, simply "spin the jar" to get a random suggestion based on your preferences.

---

## 2. Getting Started

### Sign Up
1.  Navigate to the landing page.
2.  Click the **"Get Started"** button.
3.  Enter your name (e.g., "Alex").
4.  Enter your email address and a secure password.
5.  **Location:** Enter your city (e.g., "New York, NY"). This helps us suggest relevant ideas.
6.  Click **"Create Jar"** and choose your type:
    - **Group (Social):** For friends, family, or teams.
    - **Personal:** For yourself.

**Note:** You can create and join multiple jars! Switch between them easily from the dashboard.

**Free Trial:** New users get a 14-day free trial of all Premium features!

## 3. Managing Jars (Group Mode)

**Date Jar** now supports multiple jars! You can have one dedicated Romantic jar for your partner, and multiple Group jars for different friend groups.

### Switching Jars
- On the Dashboard, click the **Jar Name/Icon** in the top header.
- A dropdown will appear showing all your memberships.
- Click a jar to switch your active view.

### Creating a New Jar
1. Open the Jar Switcher dropdown.
2. Click **"Create New Jar"**.
3. Name it (e.g., "The Boyz", "Brunch Club") and select "Group".
4. *Note: You can only have ONE Romantic jar at a time.*

### Joining an Existing Jar
1. Open the Jar Switcher dropdown.
2. Click **"Join Existing Jar"**.
3. Enter the 6-character code provided by the jar's admin.

## 4. The Dashboard

Once logged in, you will see your **Dashboard**.
- **The Jar:** A 3D representation of your jar. It fills up as you add ideas!
- **Level Banner:** Displays your current Decision Level, progress bar, and Trophy Case.
- **Stats:** Shows how many ideas are currently in the jar.
- **Action Buttons:** "Add Idea", "Spin Jar", "Weekend Plan", "Dining Concierge", "Bar Concierge".
- **Lists:**
    - **In the Jar:** Active ideas waiting to be picked.
    - **Past Dates:** Ideas you have already completed.
    - **Memories:** View your photo gallery of past dates (accessible via the main menu).

## 4. Adding Date Ideas

You can add future ideas or record past memories.

### Add Manual Memory
Want to add a date you already went on?
1. Go to the **Memories** page.
2. Click **"Add Manual Memory"**.
3. Fill in details and attach photos from your device or **Google Photos**.


### Manual Entry
1. Click the **"Add Idea"** button (Plus icon) on the dashboard.
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Description:** A short title (e.g., "Mini Golf").
   - **Details:** Optional extra info.
   - **Category:** Activity, Meal, or Event.
   - **Duration:** Estimated time.
   - **Cost:** Estimated budget.
   - **Energy:** Activity level.
   - **Time:** Day or Evening.
   - **Setting:** Indoor or Outdoor.
3. Click **"Add to Jar"**.
   - **Reward:** You earn **+15 XP** for every idea added!

### Surprise Me
Stuck on what to do? The assistant can generate custom date ideas tailored to your location and interests.
1. Click the **"Surprise Me"** button (Sparkles icon) on the dashboard.
2. Set your preferences (Category, Price, Energy).
3. Click **"Create Secret Surprise Idea"**.
4. The idea is quietly added to your jar as a "Hidden Surprise". Its details will remain secret from *both* of you until it is selected by spinning the jar!

## 5. Spinning the Jar

Ready for a date? Click **"Spin Jar"** on the dashboard to let fate decide!
**Reward:** You earn **+5 XP** just for spinning/picking a date.

### Filters
You can narrow down the random selection to fit your current mood using filters (Category, Duration, Cost, Energy, Time).

### The Reveal & Next Steps
Once the jar picks an idea:
1.  **Accept Date:** Click "Close & Enjoy!".
2.  **Set Date:** Click **"Set Date for Memory"** to schedule it.
3.  **Find Places:** For generic ideas, find specific venues nearby.

## 6. Levels & XP (Gamification)

Track your journey by earning **Experience Points (XP)** and unlocking **Trophies**!

### How to Earn XP
Everything you do in the jar helps you grow:
*   **Add an Idea:** +15 XP
*   **Spin the Jar:** +5 XP
*   **Rate a Date (Complete):** +100 XP (The biggest reward!)

### Decision Ranks
Your progress is shown on the Dashboard. As you earn XP, you unlock new titles:
1.  **Newbie Decider** (0 XP)
2.  **Curious Picker** (100 XP)
3.  **Choice Explorer** (300 XP)
4.  **Activity Planning Pro** (600 XP)
5.  **Decision Maker** (1000 XP)
6.  **Master of Choices** (1500 XP)
...and more!

### Trophy Case
Unlock special badges by achieving milestones. Collect them all to fill your cabinet!
*   **Spark Starter:** Add your first idea.
*   **The Architect:** Add 10 ideas to the jar.
*   **Idea Machine:** Add 25 ideas.
*   **Roll the Dice:** Spin the jar for the first time.
*   **Fate Tempter:** Spin the jar 10 times.
*   **Memory Maker:** Complete and rate your first date.
*   **Going Steady:** Complete 5 dates.
*   **Soulmates:** Complete 20 dates.

## 8. Concierge Services

### Dining Concierge
1.  Click **"Dining Concierge"** (Utensils icon).
2.  Enter location, cuisine preferences, and budget.
3.  Browse top-rated picks with **Google Ratings**.
4.  **"Go Tonight":** Instantly lock it in.

### Bar Concierge
1.  Click **"Bar Concierge"** (Wine icon).
2.  Find top-rated bars and lounges near you.

## 9. Weekend Planner

Need a plan for the whole weekend?
1.  Click **"Weekend Plan"** (Calendar icon).
2.  The planner generates a list of 5 activities tailored to your location.
3.  Add ideas directly to your jar.

## 10. Date Night Planner

Want a complete evening plan?
1.  Click **"Plan Date Night"**.
2.  Get a timeline with **Drinks**, **Dinner**, and an **Event**.
3.  Customize venues using "Regenerate" or "Edit".
4.  View on Map or "Go Tonight".

## 11. Managing History

### Past Dates
Scroll down on the dashboard to see **Past Dates**.
- **Rate:** Click the stars to rate how much you enjoyed the date.
    - **Note:** Rating a date marks it as "Complete" and awards **+100 XP**!
- **Add Memories:** Upload photos from your device or **Google Photos** to cherish the moment forever.
- **Repeat:** Copy an idea back into the jar.
- **Delete:** Remove the entry permanently.

### Deletion Log
See a record of deleted items via **Settings > View Deletion History**.

## 12. Settings & Configuration

### Profile Settings
- **Jar Location:** Default search area.
- **Interests:** Used for "Surprise Me" suggestions.

### Partner Management
- **Invite Partner:** Find your invite link/code in **Settings**.
- **Manage Partner:** View or regenerate codes.

### Danger Zone (Creator Only)
- **Empty Jar:** Delete all ideas/history.
- **Delete Partner:** Remove partner from the jar.
