## Scene 3: The Dashboard & Adding Ideas
**Visual:**
- Landing on the Dashboard.
- Mouse hover over the 3D Jar (it floats/interacts).
- Click the "+" (Add Idea) button.
- *Action:* Type "Mini Golf" into the Quick Add field.
- Click "Add to Jar".
- *Action:* Open "Wizard/Advanced" mode (if available) or "Concierge".
- *Action:* Open "Dining Concierge" (Magic Wand).
- Select "Asian Fusion" -> "Moderate Price".
- Click "Get Recommendations".
- Add one suggestion to the jar.

**Narrative / Subtitles:**
> "This is your Jar. It's where your best ideas live."
> "Add your own ideas in seconds..."
> "...or let our AI Concierge discover top-rated spots near you."
> "From hidden gem restaurants to local events, the Jar never runs out of inspiration."
